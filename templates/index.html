FundraiserGPT

A ChatGPT assistant for fundraisers

Start
FundraiserGPT empowers fundraisers by automating and simplifying the content creation process, providing high-quality, review-ready drafts for fundraising campaigns.

Say goodbye to the time-consuming task of drafting fundraising content from scratch and let FundraiserGPT boost your fundraising success.

Features:

  1. ChatGPT Integration: FundraiserGPT leverages the power of ChatGPT, an advanced language model, to generate review-ready drafts of content for fundraising campaigns.
  2. Prompt Engineering Made Easy: FundraiserGPT handles the heavy lifting in prompt engineering, eliminating the need for extensive manual input and enabling quick content generation.
  3. Appeal-Builder Wizard: A built-in Appeal-Builder wizard guides you through the content creation process. It asks targeted questions about your campaign's topic, audience, and urgency, and incorporates your charity or nonprofit's fundraising mission to generate review-ready copy.
  4. Versatile Content Formats: FundraiserGPT provides content in a variety of formats suitable for different platforms. It generates drafts for emails, social media posts, and direct mail materials, saving you time and effort.
  5. Image and Hashtag Suggestions: Alongside the written content, FundraiserGPT offers suggestions for compelling images and relevant hashtags to enhance your campaign's visual and social media impact.
  6. Cited Sources: FundraiserGPT ensures transparency and credibility by citing all the sources it uses in generating the content, allowing you to provide proper attribution and maintain trust.
  7. Fine-Tuning Capabilities: The tool enables you to fine-tune the generated results to align them more precisely with your specific fundraising campaign's requirements, ensuring personalized and tailored content.
  8. Simplified Content Generation: FundraiserGPT streamlines the process of utilizing ChatGPT for fundraising appeals, making it accessible and user-friendly even for those without extensive AI or writing experience.
