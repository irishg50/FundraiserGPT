FundraiserGPT

A ChatGPT assistant for fundraisers

Start
FundraiserGPT automates and simplifies the content creation process, providing high-quality, review-ready drafts for fundraising campaigns.

Features:

  1. Simplified Content Generation: FundraiserGPT streamlines the process of utilizing ChatGPT for fundraising appeals, making it accessible and user-friendly even for those without extensive AI or writing experience.
  2. ChatGPT Integration: FundraiserGPT leverages the power of ChatGPT, an advanced language model, to generate review-ready drafts of content for fundraising campaigns.
  3. Prompt Engineering Made Easy: FundraiserGPT handles the heavy lifting in prompt engineering, eliminating the need for extensive manual input and enabling quick content generation.
  4. Appeal-Builder Wizard: A built-in Appeal-Builder wizard simplifies the content creation process by asking targeted questions about your campaign's topic, audience, and urgency, and incorporating your charity or nonprofit's fundraising mission to generate review-ready copy.
  5. Versatile Content Formats: FundraiserGPT provides content in a variety of formats suitable for different platforms, including email appeals, social media posts, and direct mail letters, saving you time and effort.
  6. Image and Hashtag Suggestions: Alongside the written content, FundraiserGPT offers suggestions for compelling images and relevant hashtags to enhance your campaign's visual and social media impact.
  7. Fine-Tuning: Fine-tune your results to align them more precisely with the specific tone, style and context for your fundraising campaign.
